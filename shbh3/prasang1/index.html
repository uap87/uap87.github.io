SHBH Sabha 3

Interactive Game for Prasang 1

Scenario 1

You’re a college student who has been away from home for months and you’re coming back home for the weekend. Your friends from high school have decided to hold a hang out after months of not seeing each other. Before you come home, you find out that there is a priority sabha being held at mandir this week as well, and one of your mandir friends asks if you’re going.

Only See Friends

Rationale: You planned the hangout with your friends well in advance and you haven’t seen them in a while and plus, you attend the sabhas near your college, so it's just one weekend that you’ll be missing, you’ll be fine.

Only Go to Sabha

Rationale: Priority sabhas don’t happen that often and if it’s priority then that means it must be somewhat important. You’ll be able to see your friends next time you come home or during the summer, mandir comes first.

Meet Friends and Leave Early for Sabha

Rationale: You realize your friends are planning on meeting up an hour before sabha starts so you can go see them, leave early, and still make it for all of sabha. You get the best of both worlds.

Meet Friends and Go to Sabha Late

Rationale: You could go hang out with your friends and stay until everyone else leaves because you won’t be able to see them again for a while. This way you’ll still be able to make it for the main presentation in sabha, so you still get all of the information: you will only miss Dhoon and Prarthana.
