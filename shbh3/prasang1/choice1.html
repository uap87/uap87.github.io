SHBH Sabha 3

Interactive Game for Prasang 1

Scenario 2 - Choice 1

When you reach home, your parents reveal that one of your relatives is having a housewarming party the day of sabha as well. You know that all of your cousins will be there, and you always have fun when you are all together. Later that night, you get a text from your sanchalak/sanchalika stating that one of the singers is sick; he/she wants you to take their place in sabha. They also request you to come to mandir a bit early to practice the dhoon beforehand.

Only See Friends

You really want to see your friends because it’s been so long since you’ve hung out with them, skip the family event and only see your friends.

Only Go to Family Event

You haven’t seen your cousins in a while either and it’ll be nice seeing your other relatives too with their new house, skip your hangout with friends and go to the family event

See Friends and then Leave for Family

You can go see your friends and then leave to go to the family event late with a separate car, your parents are against this because it would be a waste of gas to not go together, but it allows you to go to both the hangout and the family event

Drop Everything and Sing in Sabha

You have second thoughts: you decide to go to sabha and agree to sing.
