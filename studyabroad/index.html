The Cotswolds

The Cotswolds are located in south central England which contain the Cotswold Hills, a range of rolling hills. They are famous for their English market towns and villages built from honey-coloured stone. Many movies have also been filmed in the Cotswolds such as Downton Abbey, the Harry Potter films, etc.

Brighton

Brighton is a beach town and a seaside resort locarted on the south coast of England. It is one of the more popular destinations for tourists due to its location, diverse communities, shopping areas, and a large cultural, music and arts scene. It is also home to the Royal Pavillion, a former royal residence that was built as a retreat for King George IV.
